Hi! I’m Surita

Thanks for visiting my portfolio!
A little about me: I recently recieved my Masters in Information at the University of Michigan, where my focus was on Human Computer Interaction & User Experience. I currently live in Seattle, where I work remotely as a UX Intern at Evva Health Inc. I have a passion for technical and educational writing, and I am curious how language can be combined with UX Methodology to improve discoverability, particularly for mobile products.


My Approach...

My background in scientific research and technical education taught me to communicate complex processes with precision and care. In UI, I combine affordances and signifiers with concise language to guide the user. In practice I frequently use information architecture, qualitative analysis, user testing, and surveys to inform my design solutions.
When I am not working, you can find me at the beach, Trader Joe's, or enjoying the Pacific Northwest's sights.
